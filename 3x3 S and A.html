3x3 Matrix Addition and Subtraction

This is exactly the same as 2x2, just with an extra row and column. The final matrix will be a 3x3, the same as the orginals.


If we have Matrices A and B


A          B
3 1 9
2 0 0
1 2 4
5 7 1
3 2 9
1 1 2

For Addition we would use the following maths.

3+5 1+7 9+1
2+3 0+2 0+9
1+1 2+1 4+2

Giving us the result

8 8 10
5 2 9
2 3 6

For Subtraction we would use the following maths.

3-5 1-7 9-1
2-3 0-2 0-9
1-1 2-1 4-2

Resulting in;

-2 -6 8
-1 -2 -9
0 1 2
