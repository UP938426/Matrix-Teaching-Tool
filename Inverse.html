Why do we need to know how to do the inverse of matrices?

As you can divide matrices you need to have a different way to achieve a similar effect. For example we have XA = B, we want to find matrix X and we know A and B. If we multiply both sides by A-1 we get XAA-1 = BA-1. As AA-1 = 1 we are left with X = BA-1, allowing us to then find X.
